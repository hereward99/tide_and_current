About

A converted warehouse on
the docks — still becoming.

Tide & Current occupies a former working warehouse on the docks at Marina Square, floating at the intersection of industry and imagination. The building is still in transformation — we think that's appropriate. The best studios are never quite finished.

Founded in 2025 by Rosa Marquez and Max Guest, Tide & Current was conceived as a space for serious work: long-term artist residencies, photographic production, and community programming that resists the ephemeral. We're especially interested in work that takes time.

The building retains its industrial bones — corrugated iron, perspex, hardwood, steel, the smell of salt air through the windows. We've added what was missing: a fully equipped analog darkroom, a large open studio with north light, and the kind of quiet that's increasingly hard to find in a city.

Spaces

Three rooms. Serious infrastructure.

◈
The Open Studio
A 2,200 sq ft open-plan workspace with high north-facing clerestory windows (with full blackout capability) and acrylic floors. Available to resident artists and by arrangement. High ceilings. No distractions.
◍
The Darkroom
A fully equipped analog darkroom with enlargers for 35mm, medium and large formats, a film processing suite, and a print drying room. Chemical mixing station. Temperature-controlled water supply. Available by membership and residency.
◇
The Water Room
An intimate event and critique space facing the inlet — floor-to-ceiling glass, a long oak table, seating for eighteen. Available for portfolio reviews, talks, screenings, and private events by inquiry.

Residency programme
"We're not interested in fast art."

Tide & Current offers two annual residencies of six months each, with a small monthly stipend, studio access, darkroom access, and one public presentation at the close of the term. We accept applications in painting, photography, mixed media, textile, and practices that don't fit categories.

Applications for the 2026-2027 cycle are now open. We select two artists per year. The application is short. The commitment is real.
